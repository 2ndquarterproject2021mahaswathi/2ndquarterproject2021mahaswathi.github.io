Grass strokes

To do this, you need to take a pointed brush number 2. Then, gently take the brush from bottom of the page to the top. When doing this, give more pressure at the bottom and keep decreasing the pressure till you reach 0. This will help you get the grass strokes very easily.

Tissue paper technique

For this one, take a wastepaper and make it into a ball. Apply a layer of any type of paint in the paper. Now take the ball and dip it in a different type of paint to make it look attractive. Take the dipped ball and press it on the paper. Do it multiply times, and you will an amazing mater piece at the end.

Cloud technique

Take at least 2 different color of highlighters. Take one of the tips of the marker and press on top of another tip of the marker and wait until color gets dissolved. Now when you highlight your notes, it will have transitional colors.

Highlighter technique

To get this effect, you need a sponge and white paint. Dab the sponge in white then press it in different areas but closer together to get the cloud effect. You can also use different color for contrast. Do this many time to get the cloud and the sky look.
