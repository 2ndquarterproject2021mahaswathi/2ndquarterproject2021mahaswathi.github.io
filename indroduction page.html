What is Art?

Art is a way of expressing thoughts and ideas into a paper. Art can convey emotion, feeling and concepts. Art can bring any immagination to life and bring joy for others.

Why do art?

Art can be challenging, but trying everyday and getting help from professionals, definetely will help you make a better art masterpiece, improves your creativity and manages your anger and stress level. By doing art, you can feel peace and calmness in you.
