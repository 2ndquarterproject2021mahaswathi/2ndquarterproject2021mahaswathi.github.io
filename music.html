The Art of Music

Music is one of the art forms. It is also part of a smaller category of art forms called Performing Arts. Performing arts is a collection of arts in which people use voices, their bodies, or other objects to convey artistic expression, usually to convey emotion. Some other examples of performing arts are:

  • Mime
  • Dance
  • Theater
  • Magic
  • Public Speaking

What is music?

Music is an organized collection of sounds. Many ancient cultures have used science and math to create different frequencies(pitches). Then, we can combine pitches to create pleasant sounds.

You can create music using instruments, such as the piano, guitar, and violin, your voice, or using everyday objects (if you're especially talented.) In this section, I will share about the 3 types of instruments (band, orchestra, and percussion) and about singing. Finally, I will list my sources.
