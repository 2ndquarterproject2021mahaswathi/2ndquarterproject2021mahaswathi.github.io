Instruments

A band is a collection of different instruments. These instruments are part of two different types of instruments - woodwind, and brass. Some woodwind instruments include:

  • Flute
  • Clarinet
  • Saxaphone


Brass instruments include

  • Tuba
  • Trombone
  • Trumpet

Famous Bands

There were (and still are) several famous bands.

  • The Beatles
  • Queen
  • Imagine Dragons
